This is me.

A massive welcome to my website! Truly. This section is going to detail probably too much personal information rather than just facts and figures. My goal is for you to be able to understand more about me as a person rather than the boring stuff that you can find on a resume.

Just like most people, I could tell you that I work hard and show up and so on... But in reality, there is one thing that you should know:

When I do something, I do it because I care about it.

Many people forget that effort and work becomes hollow when they do not strive towards a goal because of their true interest. If I choose to work on something or pursue a career with you, then you know I am truly interested.
